About Us

JamTech was founded on January 11, 2010, by Jordan Johnson to supply the general public with computers and computer accessories via the internet. He chose e-commerce as his method of delivery so that all Jamaicans with internet access would be able to be a customer and have all of their tech needs met. Technology has been growing in demand over the years and spiked when the Covid-19 pandemic hit. As bad as Covid-19 is, it has been great for business due to people having to buy computers to work and study from home. We here at JamTech are so happy to be able to meet the needs of our customers. We have been ranked the #1 tech store by the Jamaican gleaner in 2020 for top customer service and prices. In the photo to the right is JamTech's founder Jordan Johnson.
